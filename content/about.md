# Hi 👋 I’m Aaron!

I’m a Data Platform Engineer at [Tradeweb](https://www.tradeweb.com), where I work on the data infrastructure that powers the business. I’m drawn to systems under the hood — storage engines, query processing, and the trade-offs that make data fast and reliable at scale. Before Tradeweb, I worked at [Arm](https://www.arm.com), [Red Hat](https://www.redhat.com), and [Shopee](https://www.sea.com/products/shopee).

I earned my MS in Computer Science & Engineering at [UC San Diego](https://cse.ucsd.edu/) and my BA in Computer Science & Economics at [Boston University](https://www.bu.edu/cs/), where I did database systems research at the [DiSC Lab](https://disc.bu.edu) on log-structured storage. I’ve lived in [Singapore](https://maps.app.goo.gl/7231HV3MooqW8DZj7) and the U.S., and I speak English and 中文.

This blog is where I think out loud about anything that keeps me up at night.
