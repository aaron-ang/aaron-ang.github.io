### Self-help and Mental Health Community Mobile App

Won **Best UI/UX Designed Application** at [BostonHacks 2021](https://bostonhacks-2021.devpost.com/).

COVID quarantine and its resulting social isolation have taken a toll on our mental health. With Mental Health Hub (MealthHub), we aimed to alleviate negative thoughts with science-backed tools such as journaling, community-building, and speaking with a mental health professional among others. We also focused on creating a neat UI to allow users to feel at ease when using the app.

Built alongside three other students in under 24 hours.

Tech stack: [Sketch](https://www.sketch.com/), [React Native](https://reactnative.dev/), [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/).
