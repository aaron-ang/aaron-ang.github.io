### Create, join, and be informed of upcoming events in your area!

Final Project for [Codepath’s Android Development Course](https://www.codepath.org/courses/android-development).

Selected to present at Demo Day to an audience of about 80 students and judges.

Tech Stack: Kotlin, [Parse](https://docs.parseplatform.org/android/guide/), [Retrofit](https://square.github.io/retrofit/), [Glide](https://bumptech.github.io/glide/).
