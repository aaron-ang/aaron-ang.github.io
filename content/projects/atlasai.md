Won **Best Use of Convex** at [Cal Hacks 10.0](https://cal-hacks-10.devpost.com/).

Wearables have given us an abundance of health data, but most of us do little with it.

AtlasAI bridges that gap. It analyzes each user’s long-term health data and daily schedule to suggest concrete adjustments — flagging the times of day you tend to be stressed, for instance, and recommending breaks. Over time, those prompts help users trace the root causes of their stress and act on them.

Built alongside three other students in 24 hours.

Tech stack: Next.js, Typescript, Material UI, Tailwind, Python, [Convex](https://www.convex.dev/), [TogetherAI](https://together.ai/), [TerraAPI](https://tryterra.co/).
