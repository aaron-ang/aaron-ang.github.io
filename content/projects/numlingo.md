### A number-recall game for language learners

Numlingo drills active recall of numbers in the language you're learning: 60 seconds to translate as many randomly generated numbers as you can, as accurately as you can. Play solo, or join a multiplayer lobby for your language and race others.

Real-time communication is powered by the Colyseus multiplayer framework which is a wrapper around Express.js. The client and server establish a web socket connection under the hood for communications.

Built at [HackHarvard2023](https://hackharvard-2023.devpost.com/) in a team of two in under 36 hours.

Tech stack: Next.js, Typescript, Tailwind, [Zustand](https://zustand-demo.pmnd.rs/), [Colyseus](https://docs.colyseus.io/), [Vercel](https://vercel.com), [Render](https://render.com/).
