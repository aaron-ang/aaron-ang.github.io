### Marvel at celestial bodies from anywhere!

This portable kit, along with a basic telescope, offers a cheap and simple way to observe planets. Simply choose the planet you want to observe in our app. The app will calculate the necessary adjustments based on your current location and trigger the gears in the hardware to rotate. Once that's done, align your telescope with the pivot and the planet swings into view.

Built alongside three other students in 24 hours at [Hack@Brown 2024](https://2024.hackatbrown.org/).

Tech stack: React Native, [Arduino](https://www.arduino.cc/reference/en/), 3D printing.
