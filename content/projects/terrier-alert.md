### A subscription and notifier service for Boston University’s undergraduate courses.

Originally created as a personal web scraping tool for college courses, I decided to make the service publicly available by designing and implementing additional functionality over Winter 2022.

Additional improvements were made in Spring 2023 to make the system more robust. Any feedback is welcome via the [Telegram Bot](https://t.me/BUClassBot).

The service is free to use, running on [Render](https://render.com/) and MongoDB Atlas's free tier.

Tech stack: Python, Selenium, [Telegram](https://python-telegram-bot.org/), [MongoDB Atlas](https://www.mongodb.com/atlas), [Render](https://render.com/).
