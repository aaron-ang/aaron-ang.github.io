Over **70%** of people worldwide struggle to manage their daily tasks effectively and often feel overwhelmed. Acting as a personal accountability companion, Accountabud helps users prioritize what truly matters while staying focused and productive. With its human-like AI voice assistant, Accountabud engages in conversations about goals, distractions, and motivational drivers. It actively supports users through proactive interventions, such as friendly calls when distractions are detected, motivational nudges for activities like gym sessions or project work, and even escalating to a trusted friend when persistent procrastination is noticed.

Looking ahead, Accountabud aims to expand with features that simplify daily task management further, ensuring productivity and stress-free days by keeping users consistently supported, motivated, and accountable.

Built at [LA Hacks 2024](https://lahacks.com) alongside three other students in a span of 24 hours.

Tech stack: Nextjs, FastAPI, Python, Gemini, MongoDB, [Ngrok](https://ngrok.com), [Retell AI](https://www.retellai.com)
