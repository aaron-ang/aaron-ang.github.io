Won **1st Place** at [YHack 2024](https://yhack-2024.devpost.com/).

Tidbits transforms any lecture (audio or visual) into fun, byte-sized shorts with a one-click upload. We leverage what students are already familiar with: short-form video series with a core scroll-based functionality popularized by TikTok. Our goal is to transform these traditionally “unhealthy” and ”unintentional” forms of entertainment into deliberate methods of active learning.

Built alongside three other students in a span of 24 hours.

Tech Stack: Figma, Next.js, Sass, [FastAPI](https://fastapi.tiangolo.com/), [Supabase](https://supabase.com/).

AI Libraries

- [Whisper](https://openai.com/research/whisper) for speech-to-text
- [Claude](https://www.anthropic.com/claude) for script generation
- [Eleven Labs](https://elevenlabs.io/text-to-speech) for text-to-speech
- [D-ID](https://www.d-id.com/speaking-portrait/) for speaking portrait
- [DALL-E](https://openai.com/research/dall-e) for image generation
- [Suno AI](https://www.suno.ai/) for music generation
