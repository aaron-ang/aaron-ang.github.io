### A transcription and filtering tool for YouTube videos.

Let’s face it. Content moderation is difficult, and social media sites have not done a great job. Safe Transcribe is a simple [Chrome Extension](https://developer.chrome.com/docs/extensions/) that converts a YouTube video to a text file while filtering harmful speech. Furthermore, a notification is displayed if harmful or hate speech is detected. This is an accessible and handy tool for determining if the content is safe to consume.

Moreover, this tool can also help those who are time-pressed and unable to sit through an entire video. This applies to tutorials, presentations, and other long-form content where users want to quickly revisit key ideas without necessarily rewatching the whole video.

Built at [HackHarvard 2022](https://hackharvard2022.devpost.com/) in a team of two within 36 hours

Tech stack: Typescript, Express, HTML/CSS, [AssemblyAI](https://www.assemblyai.com/docs/).
